# General Rules
I have no formal training on how to draw electrical schematics, and I do not have much experience. I do not need my schematics to be perfect, but I want to develop readable and meaningful diagrams while learning good practices. Thus this is a guide to the general rules I am attempting to follow or learn along the way

## Key Rules
- Whatever you do, be consistent
  
## Flow Rules
- Higher voltages near the top, logical flow left to right.
- Power connections up to positive and down to negative/ground.
