# PSU Breakout
A method is required to get individual access to the connections from the PSU.
I do not yet have a good idea about how using a modular versus non-modular power supply will affect this decision.
## Thoughts
If I use a nonmodular powersupply, it will likely be easiest to just cut the atx motherboard adapter, and connect the wires directly to the BNC connectors and Buck Module. However, if I use a nonmodular, I have to decide between at ATX pinnout board, or cutting my modular power supply cable. I think it is not viable to use an atx pinnout board, because that will mean leaving the ATX motherboard cable intact. It is a long, dense, and unruly cable that will take up a large amount of space.
